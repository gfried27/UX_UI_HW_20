CASE STUDY

Sparking engagement among passionate young nature lovers with a little known, yet vast and vital urban wildlife refuge.

CLIENT: Minnesota Valley Refuge Friends (MVR Friends)
The official non-profit partner of the federally managed Minnesota Valley National Wildlife Refuge.

TEAM: 4 UX/UI Designers

SCOPE: Responsive website redesign, brand redesign
Present high-fidelity, clickable prototypes for desktop and mobile of the homepage, volunteer flow, and donate flow.

TOOLS: Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Google Sheets, Google Slides, Trello, Slack, tl;dv

OVERVIEW: Transform MVR Friends online presence for enhanced engagement and support
Aim to improve the UI, IA, and brand presence/cohesion in order to attract a younger audience and increase engagement in volunteering, donations, event attendance, and social media interaction.

TIMELINE: 3 weeks
